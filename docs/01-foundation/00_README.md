# Draft — README

## Snapshot
- **One-sentence concept:** A **worker-led service team OS** for small Filipino customer support teams that turns inbound tickets into structured work, routes them with visible logic, and shows worker contribution clearly.
- **Target user:** Team lead or operator of a **5–20 person Filipino customer support team** using lightweight tools and manual assignment habits.
- **Core pain:** Ticket intake, categorization, routing, and QA are inconsistent and manager-heavy, while workers have little visibility into how work gets assigned.
- **Primary track:** Future of Work
- **Optional secondary track:** Collective Prosperity
- **Current confidence level:** Medium
- **Biggest unknowns:**
  - Whether **worker-led** is immediately legible to judges without sounding ideological.
  - Whether **contribution visibility** feels empowering rather than like soft surveillance.
  - Whether the routing explanation lands as operational clarity, not just manager software.
  - Whether live AI is worth the demo risk versus seeded outputs.
- **One-sentence MVP promise:** Show one end-to-end flow where customer support tickets are ingested, classified, routed with clear reasons, reviewed by a human when confidence is low, and summarized in a contribution visibility dashboard.

## Working status
- **Stage:** Idea
- **Next checkpoint:** 4 hours

## Locked decisions
- Lead pitch sentence with **worker-led service team OS**
- Demo use case is **customer support**
- MVP is limited to:
  1. AI-assisted ticket intake
  2. Categorization
  3. Routing
  4. Human review when confidence is low
  5. Contribution Visibility
- **Do not** expand into a marketplace, full helpdesk, or full cooperative governance system.
- **Do not** introduce a fairness score unless it becomes clearly justified.

## Recommended direction
Keep the product story brutally simple:
- **Screen 1:** Intake + classification
- **Screen 2:** Routing outcome + review queue
- **Screen 3:** Contribution Visibility

## Explicit cuts
- No BPO marketplace
- No payroll or billing
- No worker voting system
- No client CRM
- No omnichannel integration
- No “AI agent” theater
